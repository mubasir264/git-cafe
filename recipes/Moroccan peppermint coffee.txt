Ingredients:

1 cup of strong brewed coffee
Fresh peppermint leaves or 1/4 teaspoon of peppermint extract
Sugar or honey to taste
Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

Brew a cup of strong coffee.
If using fresh peppermint leaves, muddle a few leaves in the bottom of the cup to release their flavor. If using peppermint extract 
add it directly to the brewed coffee.
Pour the hot coffee over the muddled peppermint leaves or mix it with the peppermint extract.
Sweeten with sugar or honey to your liking.
Top with whipped cream if desired and garnish with a sprig of fresh peppermint.
Enjoy your Moroccan Peppermint Coffee!
