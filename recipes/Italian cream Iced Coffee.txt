Italian Cream Iced Coffee is a delicious and refreshing drink. Hereâ€™s a simple recipe to make it at home:

Ingredients:

1 cup of brewed coffee, cooled
1/2 cup of milk or cream
2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
Ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional)
Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Brew a cup of strong coffee and allow it to cool.
In a glass, combine the cooled coffee, milk or cream, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract.
Stir well until all the ingredients are fully combined.
Fill the glass with ice cubes.
Top with whipped cream if desired and garnish with chocolate shavings or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
Serve immediately and enjoy your Italian Cream Iced Coffee!
You can adjust the sweetness by adding more or less sweetened condensed milk according to your taste.
